# Buy Rules

## Before Buying

Check:

- Understand business
- Honest management
- Low debt
- ROE > 15%
- ROCE > 15%
- Growing profits

## How To Buy

Monthly SIP.

## Market Crash Rules

10% correction -> Normal SIP
20% correction -> Double SIP
30% correction -> Triple SIP
40%+ correction -> Deploy aggressively

Never buy because price is going up.
