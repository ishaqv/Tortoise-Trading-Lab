## 🧨 Event-Day Rule — "No Trade Zone"

**Rule:**  
Do **not** enter or hold any trade in a stock on the day of its scheduled **corporate event**, such as quarterly results, earnings announcements, board meetings, or management updates — regardless of how strong the breakout setup appears.

**Rationale:**  
Event-driven volatility is **unpredictable and news-reactive**, not technically driven.  
Breakouts often turn into fakeouts due to algorithmic reactions, spread spikes, and sentiment flips.  
Technical indicators (VWAP, EMA, RSI, MACD) lose reliability under event conditions.

**Implementation:**  
- 🔍 Always check the event calendar before initiating a trade.  
- ⛔ Skip the stock **1 trading day before** and **until 1–2 sessions after** the event.  
- 💡 Resume trading only after structure re-forms and volume normalizes post-event.

**Mindset Reminder:**  
> “I trade logic, not news. Capital preserved today funds conviction tomorrow.”
