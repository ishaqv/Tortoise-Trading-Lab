## Strict Trading Rules – No Excuses Allowed

**✅ Do not hesitate to take entry due to the fear of failure.**

Fear has no place in a rule-based system. If setup matches criteria, act.

**✅ Do not chase price if the entry order gets partially filled.**

Let it go. Add only on a healthy pullback with structure confirmation.

**✅ Move SL below two recent swing lows.**

SL is a validation tool, not a donation. Price violating this means thesis is dead — exit.

**✅ If your first trade is a loss, walk away. You're compromised and emtoional not logical any more**

**✅ NO LONG at ATH**

For an All Time High(ATH) stock there is no overhead supply, the runway is undefined and continuation is unreliable.

**✅ Do not enter or hold any trade in a stock on the day of its scheduled corporate event, such as quarterly results,
earnings announcements, board meetings, or management updates — regardless of how strong the breakout setup appears.**

Event-driven volatility is **unpredictable and news-reactive**, not technically driven.

**✅ Never short a stock that is capable of hitting the upper circuit if you cannot exit well before the broker's RMS
square-off**

- Short trades → Watch upper circuit risk.
- Long trades → Watch lower circuit risk.

The financial consequences differ:

- A long position trapped at lower circuit suffers from market risk (the price keeps falling).
- A short position trapped at upper circuit faces both market risk and settlement risk (short delivery and auction).
