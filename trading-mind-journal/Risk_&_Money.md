# Risk & Money — The Only Freedom Lever

## 1. Capital Is Oxygen

If you lose it, you stop breathing as a trader. Protect it at all costs.

**Rules to Live By:**

- Never risk what you can’t emotionally handle losing.
- 1% loss is a paper cut, 5% loss is surgery, 10% is ICU.

---

## 2. Compounding > Jackpot

Quick money builds dopamine, not wealth.  
Slow money builds structure, peace, and freedom.

Every 3R trade adds to your confidence bank more than your equity curve.

---

## 3. Uncertainty Is the Rent

You don’t own profits — you rent them from the market, daily.  
Accept drawdowns as rent payments for staying in the game.

---
> “The only traders who survive are those who fall in love with risk management, not returns.”
