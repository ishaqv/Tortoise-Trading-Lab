# HTF Supply Detection

How to mark HTF supply properly (do this every time)
✅ Step-by-step:

1. Go to 1H

Ask:
“Where did price last fall hard without looking back?”

2. Mark: the base of that move

---

### **1. Go to the HTF you trust (1H, 2H, 4H, or Daily).**

For intraday trading → **1H is enough**.

### **2. Look to the left. Stop at the most recent swing where:**

* price tried to go up
* failed
* reversed aggressively or stalled

That point = **HTF supply**.

### **3. Mark the entire zone around that swing high → wick to body.**

### **4. That is your real HTF resistance.

Not the last wick.
Not the last micro swing.
Only the last place with actual seller aggression.**

You’re simply answering one question:

> **"Where did buyers lose control last time?"**

Where buyers lost → sellers *won*.
Where sellers won → supply zone.

That’s it.

# ⭐ **THE 3 TYPES OF CONFIRMATION CANDLES (USE THIS EVERY DAY)**

## **1️⃣ Strong Confirmation (Auto-Trade)**

These are the obvious ones:

* Full-body bullish
* Minimal upper wick
* Close above ORB high
* Strong volume

**→ You always take the trade.**

These candles rarely fail.

---

## **2️⃣ Acceptable Confirmation (Still valid — NOT “meh”)**

### Examples:

* A doji at the breakout…
  **followed by a full-body engulfing candle.**
* A medium-body candle, not perfect but not bearish
* A small wick, small hesitation, but structure still intact
* Inside candle → next candle breaks high with strong body

These are **legitimate** confirmations.

### Rule:

**Doji is fine ONLY if the next candle confirms strongly.**

This is extremely common in ORBs:

* Breakout → hesitation → institutional engulf → trend move.

These trades are HIGH probability
(*as long as sector & HTF conditions are good*).

---

## **3️⃣ Weak Confirmation (Skip Always — TRUE "meh")**

These are the ones that kill ORB trades:

* Doji with **equal top & bottom wicks**
* Confirmation candle closes **back inside the ORB candle**
* Inverted hammer
* Bearish engulfing as “confirmation”
* Tiny body with long wick
* Candle that breaks ORB high but closes weak
* Candle that closes BELOW the breakout level

These MUST be skipped every time.

**These represent low conviction from institutions.**
