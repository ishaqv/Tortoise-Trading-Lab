# Mindset Foundation — FOMO, Greed, and Fear

## 1. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

FOMO doesn’t come from the market — it comes from the illusion that you’re *late*.  
You think: “This one could be the big move.”  
Truth: You’re not chasing the move, you’re chasing the *feeling* of not being left behind.

FOMO is silent panic. It makes you buy tops and sell bottoms.

**How to Fix It**

- Accept that missing a trade is cheaper than entering a wrong one.
- Replace urgency with observation.
- Realize: the market gives infinite opportunities, but your capital is finite.

---

## 2. Greed

Greed hides as *ambition*.  
It whispers, “Hold for 5R, you deserve more.”  
Then the market takes back everything at 3R.

You don’t need to kill greed. Just cage it with rules.

**Anchor Rule:** “If price gives me 3R, I’ll take it with gratitude.”  
Compounding 3R trades will build more wealth than chasing 10R fantasies.

---

## 3. Fear

Fear is not the enemy. It’s an indicator — that you’re exposed more than you can emotionally handle.

**When fear spikes:**

- Reduce size, not your confidence.
- Trade smaller until calm becomes your baseline.
- Remember: your goal is *stability*, not excitement.

---
> “Discipline is doing the boring thing after the exciting thing failed.”
